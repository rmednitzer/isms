# Cyber Trust Austria Platinum as candidate 33 evidence vehicle

**DEC-2026-006 Revision 1 - 2026-04-19**

*Status: approved. Owner: role:CISO. Next review: 2028-04-19.*

*Interim: legal signature pending QES integration. Approved via git-level only.*

## Context

Research confirms Platinum is recognised by the Bundesamt für Cybersicherheit as § 33 evidence. Its question catalogue maps to Impl Reg 2024/2690 with less gap than ISO 27001 alone. Not mandatory; pursuing depends on entity classification and cost-benefit.

## Decision

Cyber Trust Austria Platinum Label evaluated as candidate evidence vehicle for NISG 2026 § 33 self-declaration, in parallel to ISO 27001 certification. Decision to pursue deferred pending entity classification.

## Alternatives considered

Alternative 1: ISO 27001 alone for § 33. Rejected for entities requiring externally validated NIS2-specific evidence because ISO gaps on supply-chain specifics and incident-drill evidence in the Impl Reg.
Alternative 2: pure self-declaration. Acceptable for entities without external-validation requirement.

## Consequences

Re-evaluate during P13 after entity classification confirmed.
