# Implementation statement for A.7.14 Secure disposal or re-use of equipment

**REC-2026-714 Revision 1 (DRAFT)**

*Status: draft. Owner: role:ISMS-Manager. Next review: 2027-01-01.*

## Control

A.7.14 Secure disposal or re-use of equipment (theme: physical).

## Implementation

Items of equipment containing storage media are verified to ensure sensitive data and licensed software are removed or securely overwritten before disposal or reuse, per P-008.

## Evidence

No dedicated automated evidence task is bound to this control yet; evidence is captured manually per SOP-301 and the binding is tracked in `controls/evidence-plan.yaml`.
