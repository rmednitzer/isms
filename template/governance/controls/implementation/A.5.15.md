# Implementation statement for A.5.15 Access control

**REC-2026-515 Revision 1 (DRAFT)**

*Status: draft. Owner: role:ISMS-Manager. Next review: 2027-01-01.*

## Control

A.5.15 Access control (theme: organisational).

## Implementation

Access to information and associated assets is granted on business need, least privilege, and need to know through the access-control rules, per P-002.

## Evidence

No dedicated automated evidence task is bound to this control yet; evidence is captured manually per SOP-011 and the binding is tracked in `controls/evidence-plan.yaml`.
