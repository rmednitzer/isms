## Format per entry

### A.X.YY Control title

- Applicable: yes or no
- Justification: prose statement of why this control applies (or why it has been excluded).
- Source of requirement: risk register entry, regulatory obligation, contractual requirement.
- Implementation summary: how this control is implemented in practice (link to `controls/implementation/A.X.YY.md`).
